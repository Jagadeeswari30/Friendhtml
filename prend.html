A FAMILY WE CHOOSE

Friendship is as sweet as it is, but you should always try to make the right kind of friends who would be a strong support in helping you grow. Bad friends can make you feel good about everything you do, especially when you do something that is not right. This kind of a friendship would never help you grow to become the kind of person you are destined to be.

Good friends are one of the most wonderful treasures life can possibly give you. Friends are the family you choose. They always stand by you in every situation, no matter what.
